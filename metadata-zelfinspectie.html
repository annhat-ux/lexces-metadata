Overview

The website "Zelfinspectie Werken met Gevaarlijke Stoffen" is provided by the Inspectie SZW (Ministry of Social Affairs and Employment) of the Netherlands. It serves as a guide for businesses and organizations dealing with hazardous substances. The site offers self-inspection tools and guidelines to ensure compliance with Dutch legislation regarding safety and health standards in workplaces handling hazardous materials. Users can access resources to assess and improve workplace safety practices, protecting both workers and the environment.
