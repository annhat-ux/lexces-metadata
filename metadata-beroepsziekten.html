Overview

The Helpdesk Beroepsziekten website offers information and support related to occupational diseases. It provides resources for recognizing, reporting, and preventing work-related illnesses. The site is geared towards professionals in occupational health, employers, and employees, offering guidelines, tools, and access to expert advice to address and manage occupational diseases effectively.
